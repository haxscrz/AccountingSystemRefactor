Administration Guide

Month-End Processing Manual

This guide will specifically walk you through the process of legally starting a new accounting period. The Month-End Processing system is a powerful native utility that intelligently applies unposted journal entries to the permanent ledger, recalculates Trial Balance data, and wipes out pending queues, seamlessly allowing your chronological records to roll over into the new month.

Important Prerequisites

A Note on Unposted Transactions

Don't worry if you still have hanging unposted transactions pending in the system! You completely skip manually posting them yourself. The Month-End system is now highly advanced; it will automatically scoop them up, dynamically bulk-post them to the correct charts via the Ledger Engine, and resolve them before securely zapping the data to make way for your next month's entries!

Step-by-Step Instructions

1

Access the Month-End Processing Page

  • Make sure you have logged in securely and loaded into your correct Company context.
  • On the far-left main navigation menu, click on FS System to switch to your modules.
  • Look under the Administration section on the left sidebar and select Month-End Processing.
2

Review Current Period & Warning Flags

On the top of the Month-End page, you will spot a diagnostic card labeled Current Period Information.

  • Carefully verify the Period to Close matches the month you intend to officially close out.
  • If you have pending entries, you'll see a soft-amber warning badge under Unposted Transactions natively alerting you that a bulk ledger-commit will trigger. You may proceed!
3

Confirm Period

In the first section titled "1. Confirm Period":

  • Look exactly at the expected date format prompt next to the box (for example: 4/2026).
  • Type that exact month and year combination directly into the text box.
  • Click the blue Confirm button to unlock Phase 2.
4

Execute Ledger Ledger Posting & Close

After unlocking the module, Step 2 will unblur dynamically.

  • Double check everything one last time.
  • Click the red Execute Month-End Close button.
Warning: Unrecoverable Action

Month-End closes are completely irreversible! They rewrite your balances immediately.

5

Check the Processing Log & Finish

Once you click the execute button, a sophisticated terminal pop-up log box will appear displaying real-time events. Look for lines like "Starting month-end processing..." and "Accounts processed: XXXX".

Wait silently while it securely connects to your isolated server. Once entirely completed, a green toast notification will fade down confirming: "Month-end close completed successfully!"

You have successfully rolled over the system into the next month! The UI will instantly snap your active period to the brand new month, allowing you to freely enter transactions organically.
